FIELDTRIP

fieldtrip is a mobile application designed to help adults rediscover group learning. concieved in response to the reduction in “third spaces” in metro areas across the US, fieldtrip serves as a node for social connectivity while also offering a unique educational experience to its users. for this project, i conducted product research + development, ux design (hi-fi + lo-fi prototyping), user testing, + brand design.
